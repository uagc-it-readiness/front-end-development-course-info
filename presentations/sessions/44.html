Tips for the New Hire

Learn who to ask questions.

Grow your professional network.

Find the next thing and take ownership

Become the 'guy' or 'gal' for something.

Get involved in non-business related stuff.

Avoid politics and gossip at all costs.

Don't be a jerk to another developer, EVER!

Hosting for a client

Rule #1

Never have an invoicing system where your customer pays you.

Setup a reseller account through your hosting provider.

Rule #2

Never buy or register a domain for a customer.

You don't want to be blamed if a domain expires .

Finding a hosting provider.

Finding the right hosting provider can be just as tricky as building the website

Things to consider

  • Price per month
  • Bandwidth Usage
  • Support
  • Platform - Windows/Linux
  • Support
